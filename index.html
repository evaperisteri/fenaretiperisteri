about me

Hi everyone! My name is Fenareti. I love painting and photography and I use these mediums as a way to express myself and connect with the world. I grew up in a family with strong artistic interests I' ve always beem surrounded by creativity which has deeply influenced my work and inspired my artistic journey.
I studied at the Univercity of Ioannina in Greece at th Department of Fine Arts and Arts Sciences. "The Department of Fine Arts and Sciences of Art [...] offers expertise in the fields of Fine Arts, History and Theory of Art, Painting and Sculpture including Printmaking, analogue and digital Photography, Computer Animation, Video and Multimedia."
After graduating I 've been exploring various tecniques, always experimenting with new styles, materials and ideas. For me, art is a journey of learning, evolving, and broadening my horizons. I’m always excited to push boundaries and discover new ways to bring my vision to life.
